About Brew & Bean

Our story, our passion, our people

Our Story

Brew & Bean was born in 2015 from a single dream: to bring world-class specialty coffee to the streets of Kathmandu. Our founder, Priya Shrestha, had spent years studying coffee culture in Melbourne and Seoul before returning home with a mission.

She opened our first tiny 20-seat café in Thamel with two espresso machines, a hand-painted menu, and an unwavering belief that Nepal deserved great coffee. Within six months, the queues stretched out the door.

Today, Brew & Bean is a beloved community space — a place where students, travellers, artists, and locals all find their rhythm over an exceptional cup.

What We Stand For

🌍

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Nepal's own Ilam region. Fair wages, sustainable practices, and long-term relationships.

🎓

Barista Training

Every barista completes a 3-month in-house training programme. We invest in our team because great coffee starts with great people.

♻️

Zero Waste Goal

Compostable packaging, used-coffee-grounds composting, and a bring-your-own-cup discount. We're on a journey to zero waste.
