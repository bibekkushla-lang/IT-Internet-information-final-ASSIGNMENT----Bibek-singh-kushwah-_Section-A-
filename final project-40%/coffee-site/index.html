Welcome to Brew & Bean

Kathmandu's Most Beloved Coffee House

Nestled in the heart of Thamel, Brew & Bean has been serving exceptional coffee since 2015. We believe great coffee isn't just a drink — it's a moment of pause, a ritual, a connection. Every cup we craft honours the farmers, the roasters, and the community that makes it possible.
